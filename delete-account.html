Delete Your Account

Watch Authenticator — account and personal data deletion request

You can permanently delete your Watch Authenticator account and all associated personal data using either of the methods below. Both options remove the same data and are processed within 30 days.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (instant)

If you can sign in to the app:
  1. Open Watch Authenticator on your device.
  2. Tap your account badge in the top-right of the home screen.
  3. Tap Delete my account at the bottom of the account sheet.
  4. Type DELETE in the confirmation dialog and tap Delete.

Deletion is immediate. The app signs you out and clears all local data.

Option 2 — Email request (if you cannot access the app)

Send an email to d9designk@gmail.com
  • Subject line: Delete my account
  • Send it from the email address registered with your account so we can verify ownership.
  • We will confirm the deletion by reply within 7 business days and complete it within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account record — email and user identifier
  • All scan history and authentication sessions linked to your account
  • All AI-generated reports and authentication results
  • Locally cached photos, scan results, and preferences on your device

What may be retained

  • Purchase transaction records held by Google Play and our payment processor (RevenueCat) — these are retained where required by law or for accounting and fraud prevention. They cannot be linked back to your deleted account.
  • Aggregated, fully anonymized analytics data that cannot be tied to you personally.

Questions

For anything related to account deletion or data privacy, contact d9designk@gmail.com.
